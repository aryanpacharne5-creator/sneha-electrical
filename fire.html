Fire Protection Systems

Complete fire safety solutions for industrial, commercial and residential projects

Fire Hydrant System

A complete network of piping connected to a reliable water source to provide immediate water supply for firefighting. Hydrant pumps ensure sufficient pressure when municipal supply is inadequate, enabling fire engines to quickly control and suppress fires.

Fire Detection & Suppression

Early detection systems protect occupants and emergency responders. Automated suppression systems extinguish fires without human intervention. Options include conventional, addressable, intelligent and wireless systems.

Fire Sprinkler System

Automatic sprinklers detect heat and immediately release water to control or extinguish fire before it spreads. Distribution piping and sprinkler heads are permanently installed throughout protected areas.

Fire Pump House

Fire pumps deliver high-pressure water to hydrant and sprinkler systems. Systems are UL/FM approved and comply with NFPA-20 standards for maximum reliability during emergencies.

High Mast Erection

High mast lighting provides powerful illumination for large areas without multiple poles. Heights from 12–30m with safety accessories and aviation obstruction lights included.
